What do we know now:

The the dataset includes the data of saved tracks on Spotify for the last three years by user abstractos. And according to statistics and science, the person whose data was analyzed is unique (this can be seen from the fairly average values in the popularity column, which really means how popular the song is among all users) and amazing musical taste.

Most of the pop music variations are present, such as art pop, hyper pop, and so on. Classical music is also in the top 3 most listened to genres.

What is interesting is that the data presented largely coincides with the statistics that Spotify provides at the end of the year as Spotify Review.

The rest of the information can be found on the respective webpages.
